    About Us
    Empower a safer
    & more convenient virtual world
    In the booming web3 and metaverse, millions of people engage in virtual worlds for gaming, socializing, and collaboration. However, most of these worlds lack a secure avatar verification system, leaving users vulnerable to scams, frauds, and impersonation.
    Additionally, these worlds often operate in isolation. VerifiedX is a cross-game avatar verification platform for web3 gamers, developers, and metaverse residents. Our mission is to enhance safety, credibility,
    and convenience in and across these virtual realms.
